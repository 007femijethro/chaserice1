About Chase Rice

Chase Rice (born September 19, 1985) is an American country music singer-songwriter and former reality TV contestant. He co-wrote "Cruise" and later launched a successful solo career with hits on the Country charts.

Career Timeline

  • 2010: Runner-up on Survivor: Nicaragua.
  • 2012–2014: Songwriting success, co-wrote hits like "Cruise".
  • 2014–Present: Released multiple albums and toured internationally.
